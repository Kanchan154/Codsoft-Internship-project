Our Coffee

Espresso

Rich, full-bodied espresso with a caramel-like sweetness. Espresso is a concentrated type of coffee drink with Italian origins. Prepared from the same coffee beans as regular coffee, it’s its preparation that differentiates espresso from regular coffee.

Cappuccino

Espresso with steamed milk and a light layer of foam. One of the most popular coffee drinks globally, a cappuccino is the perfect blend of flavorful coffee and luxurious milk foam.

Latte

Espresso with steamed milk, topped with a light layer of foam. A latte (or caffè latte)is a milk coffee drink with espresso that boasts a silky layer of foam on top. A proper latte contains one or two shots of espresso, steamed milk, and a final, thin layer of frothedmilk on top. Combining these elements result in a balanced and beautiful milky coffee.
